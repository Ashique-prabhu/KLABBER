French Open

Yana Sizikova arrested for alleged match-fixing at 2020 French Open

A Russian tennis player was busted for an alleged match-fixing fraud in which she deliberately lost a doubles game at the French Open, authorities said Friday. Yana Sizikova — ranked No. 89 for doubles by the Women’s Tennis Association last year — was arrested at the tournament in Paris on Thursday and charged with “sports bribery and organized fraud” for a game she played in September, the Paris prosecutor’s office said. An investigation was opened in October by a French police unit that centered on suspicious betting patterns in the first round of a women’s doubles match at the French Open on Sept. 30, officials said.
